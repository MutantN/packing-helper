Quick rules — no quiz needed
Clothing: haven't worn it in 12+ months → donate
Duplicate tools/appliances: keep the best one, donate the rest
Books: won't re-read it, or available digitally → donate
Linen/towels: stained, worn, or a 4th+ set → bin or donate
Cables/tech: don't know what it's for → bin
Cleaning & pantry items: nearly empty or expired → use up or bin
Guilt gifts: the guilt isn't a reason to keep it — donate freely
Furniture: won't fit the new room's dimensions → measure now, sell or donate early so it's not a last-minute scramble
"What if I need it someday": if you haven't in 2 years, that day isn't coming
